Privacy Policy

16-0 collects zero personal data. There is no analytics, no tracking, and no fingerprinting. There are no user accounts and no login.

The game uses no cookies. It does use your browser's localStorage to remember a few preferences and your own game history: your light or dark theme choice, whether sound is muted, and a short list of your recent runs (game mode, result, win-loss record, and the initials of the players you drafted). This data never leaves your device, is not personal information, is not sent anywhere, and you can erase the run history any time with the "Clear" button on the main menu.

The game itself collects nothing. All player and team data is bundled with the site and runs in your browser. The game makes no calls to any analytics or tracking service.

Third-party services

To load and style the page, your browser requests a few files from third-party services. Like any web request, these can expose your IP address and basic request details to those providers. The game sends them no personal data of its own.

  • Tailwind CSS (cdn.tailwindcss.com) - styling framework loaded as a script.
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com) - the Barlow and Barlow Condensed typefaces.
  • Buy Me a Coffee - an optional donation link. It is a plain link, not an embedded widget, so your browser only contacts that site if you choose to click it.

These services are convenience-only and set no cookies through this game. They can be self-hosted later to remove the third-party requests entirely.

Data attribution

Player and team statistics are public historical facts. Our reference lineage is the openly-licensed "NBA Database" by Wyatt Walsh (Kaggle, CC BY-SA 4.0), built from the official NBA stats API via the MIT-licensed nba_api project. No proprietary or licensed database is reproduced.
