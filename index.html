  1. Pick how much you want to know: full ratings and stats, or a blind build.
  2. Choose your challenge: 16-0 (four best-of-seven rounds) or Gauntlet 7-0 (one game versus each decade's best team).
  3. Spin to reveal a real team from any era, then draft one player from it.
  4. Repeat until your starting five is complete, one per position.
  5. Win every game. A single perfect run is the whole goal.

How games are decided

Each lineup gets a strength score: the average overall of your roster, plus a small bonus for scoring punch (points per game). The two scores are compared, the home side gets a small edge, and a bit of random swing is added so upsets can happen. Later rounds face tougher opponents, so a clean 16-0 is meant to be hard.

Your browser's system setting for reduced motion is also respected automatically.
