Privacy Policy

16-0 collects zero personal data. There is no analytics, no tracking, and no fingerprinting. There are no user accounts and no login.

The game uses no cookies. It does use your browser's localStorage to remember a few preferences and your own game history: your light or dark theme choice, whether sound is muted, and a short list of your recent runs. For each run this includes the game mode, result, win-loss record, the five players you drafted (with their positions and ratings), and the historical teams you faced in that run. It also keeps running totals across your runs, such as how many you have played and the simulated per-player stat lines they produced. This data never leaves your device, is not personal information, is not sent anywhere, and you can erase all of it any time with the "Clear" button on the main menu.

The game itself collects nothing. All player and team data is bundled with the site and runs in your browser. The game makes no calls to any analytics or tracking service.

Third-party services

To load and style the page, your browser requests a few files from third-party services. Like any web request, these can expose your IP address and basic request details to those providers. The game sends them no personal data of its own.

  • Tailwind CSS (cdn.tailwindcss.com) - styling framework loaded as a script.
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com, fonts.gstatic.com) - the Barlow and Oswald typefaces.
  • Donation link (Ko-fi) - the optional "Support go16and0" link in the footer goes to Ko-fi, which handles any voluntary donations. It is a plain link, not an embedded widget, so your browser only contacts Ko-fi if you choose to click it.

These services are convenience-only and set no cookies through this game. They can be self-hosted later to remove the third-party requests entirely.
