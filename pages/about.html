About 16-0

16-0 is a browser-based basketball simulation game. Build a team, run a playoff gauntlet, and try to go a perfect 16-0.

This game is an independent fan project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the NBA, NBPA, or any team or player.

Player statistics are used for informational and historical reference and are sourced from public historical records.

16-0 is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with any other game, league, team, or company. Any resemblance to other games is limited to shared, general gameplay concepts.
